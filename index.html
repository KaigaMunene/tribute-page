Mr. Kevin Mugo

A soilder, a Father and a dear Cousin

Mugo was a hardworking, down to earth kind of person. He was a football fan like many of us and every free chance he got he would go and play the game he loved with his friends. He was always happy everytime I met him and he was always there with grandma, either chating with her or doing various chores in grandma's homestead. I for one am a man of few words, we never talked a lot with Mugo but he would always listen to me and find ways to engage me in a certain conversation. It's truly a blessing to have had him mine and yourlives, I don't know what plans God had for taking him so early but i Know he up there in heaven paving a way for all of us.For one I know he safe up there with grandpa smiling down on us. I will miss you cousin, I don't have much to say but this is a poem for you:

Dear Cousin,

Even though you are gone away,
Your love will always be here to stay.
You touched our hearts with so many things.

God knew you were the one to save,
He took you home to get some rest,
Even though we loved you best.

Our hearts are filled with so much pain,
God loved you more, there was no shame.

At this time we must let go.
Your memories we will keep a flow.
Rest our dear cousin with peace of mind,
Your memories will live on through.

To my dear cousin,
Mugo.
